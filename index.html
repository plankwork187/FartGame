📊 Character Stats Guide

All stats are rated 1–5. Higher stats always make the game harder. Lower stats = easier run. Choose wisely (or don't, we're not your mom).

💨 Smell (1–5)
How pungent your crop dust is.
⭐ Level 1 — Barely a whiff. A ghost SBD. NPCs have to bury their face in it to notice.
⭐⭐⭐ Level 3 — Rancid and present. A respectable Silent but Deadly cloud. You'd know.
⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ Level 5 — ☠️ Biological hazard. Massive, eye-watering crop dust. Tags NPCs from across the room.
Higher = harder (bigger detection radius).
⏱ Linger (1–5)
How long SBD clouds hang in the air.
⭐ Level 1 — Gone in seconds. Like it never crop-dusted at all. Very forgivable.
⭐⭐⭐ Level 3 — Hangs around awkwardly. That rancid aftertaste that won't quit.
⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ Level 5 — Eternal SBD. The cloud will outlive your dignity. NPCs walking through much later still get gassed.
Higher = harder (longer exposure window).
💥 Accident (1–5)
How likely you are to cut the cheese accidentally.
⭐ Level 1 — Iron sphincter. Zero accidental BRAPs. A true professional.
⭐⭐⭐ Level 3 — Normal gassiness. Occasionally loses the battle. Mild accidental SBDs.
⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ Level 5 — Accidental BRAPs on repeat. Involuntary crop dusting constantly. You are NOT in charge here.
Higher = harder (more random accidents).
⚡ Frequency (1–5)
How fast gas builds up passively.
⭐ Level 1 — Slow gassiness. Plenty of breathing room between SBDs.
⭐⭐⭐ Level 3 — Steady crop-dusting pressure. Manageable, but always there.
⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ Level 5 — 💨 Maximum gassiness. Release constantly or it all comes out at once. No mercy.
Higher = harder (constant pressure).
🔊 Volume (1–5)
How loud your BRAPs are.
⭐ Level 1 — Silent but Deadly. Pure SBD. Ears are safe, noses are not.
⭐⭐⭐ Level 3 — Audible crop dust. Nearby NPCs perk up. Start coughing strategically.
⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ Level 5 — 🔊 Maximum BRAP. Loud, wet, rancid. Heard from across the room. Instantly.
Higher = harder (wider sound detection radius).

How to Play

↔️Hold left or right to lean and release gas. Longer hold = more powerful (and riskier) release.
👃Smell creates a lingering cloud — bigger releases make bigger, longer-lasting clouds that can tag NPCs who walk through later.
👂Loudness is instant — anyone within hearing range reacts immediately, even from a distance, with no visible cloud.
🧍NPCs exist at three depths: foreground, midground, and background. Closer NPCs notice you more easily.
📈Reach the level's gas target before Suspicion maxes out to clear the level.
👥Some levels include a companion standing right next to you with their own special twist.
⚡Each character has a Special Move — unlocked in a future update. Watch this space!
